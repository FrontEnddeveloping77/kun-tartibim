Kundalik Kuchli Tartib

🌅 Ertalab (5:30 – 9:00)

  • 5:30 – Uyg‘onish, tahorat, subh namozi
  • 6:00 – Qur'on yoki duolar bilan kunni boshlash
  • 6:30 – Yengil jismoniy mashqlar
  • 7:00 – Nonushta
  • 7:30 – Kichik maqsadlar ustida ishlash
  • 8:30 – Kursga tayyorgarlik

📘 Kurs Kunalari (Sesh, Pay, Shan – 9:00 – 12:40)

  • To‘liq e’tibor bilan o‘rganish

☀️ Tushlik Oralig‘i (13:00 – 15:00)

  • 13:00 – Sog‘lom tushlik
  • 13:30 – Dam olish
  • 14:00 – Kursni takrorlash yoki yangi bilim

🕒 Kun O‘rtasi (15:00 – 18:00)

  • 15:00 – Loyiha, dars, takrorlash
  • 16:30 – Tashqariga chiqish, yurish
  • 17:00 – O‘z-o‘zini baholash

🌙 Kechki Vaqt (18:00 – 22:30)

  • 18:00 – Namoz, oilaviy vaqt
  • 19:00 – Kitob yoki video orqali o‘rganish
  • 20:00 – Telefonni cheklash, ongli nazorat
  • 21:00 – Duolar, Qur’on
  • 22:30 – Uxlash
