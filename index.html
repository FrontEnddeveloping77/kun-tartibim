Kundalik Kuchli Kun Tartib

🌅 Ertalab (5:30 – 9:00)

  • 5:30 – Uyg‘onish, tahorat, subh namozi
  • 6:00 – Qur'on yoki duolar bilan kunni boshlash
  • 6:30 – Yengil jismoniy mashqlar
  • 7:00 – Nonushta
  • 7:30 – Kichik maqsadlar ustida ishlash
  • 8:30 – Kursga tayyorgarlik

📘 Kurs Kunlari (Sesh, Pay, Shan – 9:00 – 12:30)

  • To‘liq e’tibor bilan o‘rganish

☀️ Tushlik Oralig‘i (13:00 – 15:00)

  • 12:35 – Sog‘lom tushlik
  • 13:10 – Dam olish
  • 14:10 – Kursni takrorlash yoki yangi bilim

🕒 Kun O‘rtasi (15:00 – 18:00)

  • 15:00 – Loyiha, dars, takrorlash
  • 16:30 – O‘z-o‘zini baholash
  • 17:25 – Tashqariga chiqish yurish, asrga borish

🌙 Kechki Vaqt (18:00 – 22:30)

  • 18:40 – Namoz, oilaviy vaqt
  • 19:00 – Kitob yoki video orqali o‘rganish
  • 19:40 – Telefonni cheklash, Ongli nazorat, shomga borish
  • 20:25 – Duolar, Qur’on, Kitob o'qish, xuftonga borish
  • 22:20 – Uxlash
